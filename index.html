Pokemon Legends: Arceus Tracker

Added March 4, 2022

This tracker is initially developed to assist keep track of the wisps in the Spiritomb quest. All the six areas where the wisps are to be found and collected by the player is completely implemented.

I do have future plans to add in the locations of all Unknown forms as well. Not sure if there is something else I want to add-in, but the possibility still optional if I want to add in something else.
